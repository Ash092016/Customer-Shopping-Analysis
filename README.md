# Customer Behavior Dashboard w/ Power BI

![Dashboard Preview](/images/Page1.png)

## Introduction

This **Customer Behavior Dashboard** is designed for **business analysts, marketing teams, and decision-makers** to gain actionable insights into customer purchasing patterns, preferences, and engagement. Using a structured retail dataset containing customer demographics, transactions, subscription status, and product categories, this dashboard provides a **centralized, interactive platform** to analyze business performance and customer trends.

The goal of this project is to help organizations **understand customer behavior, improve targeting strategies, optimize sales performance, and enhance customer experience** through data-driven decision-making.

## Dashboard Overview

### Key KPIs

* **Number of Customers** ‚Äì Displays total distinct customers
* **Average Purchase Amount** ‚Äì Indicates spending behavior
* **Average Review Rating** ‚Äì Measures customer satisfaction

These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of overall business performance.

---

### Visual Insights

* **% of Customers by Subscription Status (Donut Chart):**
  Shows the ratio of subscribed vs non-subscribed customers.

* **Revenue by Category (Column Chart):**
  Compares revenue contribution across product categories.

* **Sales by Category (Bar Chart):**
  Displays category-wise sales distribution.

* **Revenue by Age Group (Bar Chart):**
  Identifies high-value customer segments.

* **Sales by Age Group (Bar Chart):**
  Analyzes purchasing trends across age groups.

---

### Filters & Interactivity

The dashboard allows dynamic filtering using slicers:

* **Subscription Status** ‚Äì Yes / No
* **Gender** ‚Äì Male / Female
* **Category** ‚Äì Product categories
* **Shipping Type** ‚Äì 2-Day, Express, Free Shipping, Standard, Store Pickup

These filters enable users to **drill down into specific segments** and analyze trends in real-time.
